On The Spot
Mobile Pizza Bot

Our new pizza delivery technology is changing the whole delivery process. Our service eliminates the pizza shop which means a quicker more convienient experience for you the customer. Every aspect of our service is contained on the truck including the kitchen.

Secure payment system

Our new system requires no credit card information to be shared online. Our mobile systems are all equipped with cash and card reading devices, which allows the customer to pay once their order arrives.

24/7 service

No human interaction means no need for breaks or sleep, which means pizza whenever you want.

Full Automation

All pizza is made by our on board ai, which means there will be no room for human error and you recieve the perfect pie each time.

Secure Order System

We will email you a specific order code once your order is in progress. Our secure payment system paired with our order code system means your order stays your order
